üìñ How It Works

Step 1: The page-monitor.js content script checks the page URL and meta tags when a page loads.

Step 2: If keywords like "porn", "xxx", "adult", "nsfw" are detected in the URL or page title, it triggers the blur.

Step 3: The extension applies filter: blur(40px) to the entire page body, making it impossible to see.

Step 4: A warning overlay appears with options to leave the page or contact a parent.

Step 5: The incident is logged and the parent is notified in Active or Both modes.
